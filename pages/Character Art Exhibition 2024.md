<h4><strong>The theme for CAE 2024</strong></h4>
<h4><strong><em>Celebrating Diverse Excellence&nbsp;</em>|&nbsp;听见你心中的卓越</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<blockquote>
<p>
<br>Every child is either demonstrating excellence or on the path toward achieving
it.</p>
<p>每一个孩子不是正展现卓越，就是在迈向卓越的路途上</p>
</blockquote>
<p>
<br>The Hong Wen School's Character Education Art Exhibition, which began
in 2023, emphasizes the school's core value of character. Each year, the
exhibition's theme reflects one of the six core values of the school, aiming
to embody these values through art.</p>
<p>This year's theme is “Excellence”, which is also one of Hong Wen School's
core values. Every child has their own unique form of excellence. Excellence
is not limited to being the best in a group but is about striving to achieve
one's potential in the pursuit of dreams and goals.</p>
<p>Every child possesses their own strengths, with some already demonstrating
excellence while others are on the path toward it. Each of these qualities
deserves to be celebrated. We acknowledge, see, and cherish each child's
unique traits. Through the art exhibition, we encourage children to listen
to their inner sense of excellence, learn to affirm themselves, and become
confident individuals.</p>
<p></p>
<p>宏文学校品德教育美展从2023年开始回归校本核心价值——品德，以学校六大价值观作为每一年的美展主题，希冀用艺术方式体现学校价值观。</p>
<p><strong>卓越</strong>，是今年美展主题，亦是宏文学校价值观之一。</p>
<p>每个孩子都有其独特的“卓越”，卓越并不仅限于成为群体中最好的那个，而是在追求梦想与目标的过程时，孩子能尽其所能去成就自己。</p>
<p>每个孩子都有自己的长处，有些不是正展现卓越，就是在迈向卓越的路途上，都值得被庆祝，我们肯定、看见、爱惜每位孩子的特点。透过美展，鼓励孩子听见自己的心里的卓越，学习肯定自己，成为自信的自己。</p>
