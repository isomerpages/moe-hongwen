# School Hours and Dismissal

## School Hours for Students

Our school hours for students are from 7.30 a.m. to 1.35 p.m., Mondays to Fridays.

Students are encouraged to be in the hall/classroom by 7.15 a.m. for pre-assembly. They are also expected to be present at the assembly venue punctually for the daily flag-raising ceremony which begins promptly at 7.30 a.m.&nbsp;

Dismissal of students will be staggered by levels. Please refer to the table below for dismissal details.
