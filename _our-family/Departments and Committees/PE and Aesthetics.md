<h2>Key Information</h2>
<h3>PE Programme</h3>
<p>Through PE, we would like our pupils&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>to enjoy movement and physical activities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to acquire the knowledge, skills, right attitudes and values towards the
pursuit of a lifelong physically active and healthy lifestyle.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>to develop self-management skills, social and co-operative skills, and
build character.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h3>Aesthetics&nbsp;</h3>
<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>a Creative Artist(e) who Respects diversity of Expression (CARE)</p>
<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>We will provide a myriad of opportunities for pupils to express themselves
artistically and develop a lifelong appreciation for the arts of different
cultures and forms.</p>
